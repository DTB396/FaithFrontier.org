## Overview

This NLRB Region 04 matter concerns an unfair labor practice charge filed on March 4, 2025, against Hard Rock Cafe International (N.J.) Inc. and Boardwalk 1000, LLC as single/joint employers, with a related respondent listing for Hard Rock Cafe International (STP), Inc. The case remains open on the NLRB docket and reflects multiple General Counsel actions and charging party filings during March and April 2025.
